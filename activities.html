Top 5 Activites to do in Seattle During Covid

There are many activites you can do in seattle during Covid. Many of them are out door activites which are safer compare to indoor. I will talk about top 5 activites to do

1. Pike Place Market

Because of COVID-19 general Market hours have changed. Most businesses are open from 9 a.m. - 5 p.m. Some restaurants offering takeout and delivery stay open until 8 p.m. Visit PikePlaceMarket.org/GroceryShop and PikePlaceMarket.org/Takeout for individual business hours.
