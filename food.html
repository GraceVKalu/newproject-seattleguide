Seattle Resturants

Traveling may be one of the most complicated and stressful things to do, especially during a pandemic. Another challenging thing to do when traveling is finding places to eat. With the pandemic going on it makes it even harder to find places which offer a safe dine-in experience, even if you’re a local or just visiting Seattle. We have created a list of restaurants that are not only delicious but offer that safe dine in experience you’re looking for.



Lecōsho

89 University St, Seattle, WA 98101

(206) 623-2101

Price point: meal about $11-$20 per person

Yelp: ★★★★☆

Lecosho is an European styled restaurant that is famous for their high quality meats but they also have vegetarian and vegan options. It has a stylish eatery with wood-trimmed banquettes, serving seasonal cuisine & housemade sausages. They offer dine-in, takeout and delivery


Marmite

1424 11th Ave, Seattle, WA 98122

(206) 755-8606

Price point: meal about $11-$20 per person

Yelp: ★★★★☆

Marmite is a French styled restaurant that is known for their hearty soups, sandwiches & weekend brunch, using locally sourced fresh and organic ingredients. The atmosphere is rustic with a very bright and light-filled surrounding with a farm to table movement. They offer dine-in and takeout.


Sushi Kashiba

86 Pine St Suite #1, Seattle, WA 98101

(206) 441-8844

Price point: meal about $50+ per person

Yelp: About ★★★★★

Sushi Kashiba is a high-end sushi & omakase, in a chic room with water place views. Omakase means “I’ll leave it up to you” in Japanese. This means the chef can have creative license for the duration of the meal, choosing each piece of sushi for you and keeps you surprised. This restaurant offers dine-in and takeout.


Los Agaves

1514 Pike Place Ave 7, Seattle, WA 98101

(206) 504-0202

Price point: meal about $1-$10 per person

Yelp: ★★★★☆

Los Agaves is a restaurant at Pike Place Market which offers modern Mexican street food, including tamales and breakfast. They focus on giving flavorful meals and low prices for those who don’t want to break the bank in Seattle. Los Agaves provides dine-in and takeout.
